🧺 Get FarmStand on your phone

FarmStand installs straight from your browser — no app store needed, and it's tiny.

🍎iPhone (Safari): tap the Share button (square with arrow), scroll down, tap Add to Home Screen.
🤖Android (Chrome): tap the ⋮ menu, then Add to Home screen (or "Install app").

Market pins come from OpenStreetMap, a free world map built by millions of people. Market owners: tap any pin → Edit page to add your photo, products, hours and phone number — or tap ＋ Add your market if you're not on the map yet. Your edits are saved on this device.
